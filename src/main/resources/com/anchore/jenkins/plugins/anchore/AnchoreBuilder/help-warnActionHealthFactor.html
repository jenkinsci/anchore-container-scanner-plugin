The amplification factor to apply to warn actions when computing the build health score.
The default factor is 5.0
  • A factor of 0.0 means that warn actions will not influence the build health score.
  • A factor of 0.1 means that 10 warn actions result in 99% health
  • A factor of 0.5 means that 10 warn actions result in 95% health
  • A factor of 1.0 means that 10 warn actions result in 90% health
  • A factor of 2.0 means that 10 warn actions result in 80% health
  • A factor of 2.5 means that 10 warn actions result in 75% health
  • A factor of 5.0 means that 10 warn actions result in 50% health
  • A factor of 10.0 means that 10 warn actions result in 0% health
  • A factor of 25.0 means that 1 warn action results in 75% health
The factor is persisted with the build results, so changes will only be reflected in new builds.
